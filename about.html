ABOUT ISE

ISE- Institute Of Student Engineering is a Non profit Organization Registered No 391, of 2016, under Andhra Pradesh Registration Act, 2001.
It was established in the year 2016, March 3rd. Its objective is to conduct entrepreneual courses and workshops which aim to let the students understand entrepreneurship and ignite the zeal to work on entrepreneurial grounds and contribute their bit to the society.


VISION

To make every individual realize their potential.

MISSION

To provide core platform which help the students to build various abilities beyond academics like Entrepreneurial skills, soft skills, Communicational skills and many other skills

GOAL

To provide an efficient platform for the students who can come up with an idea to serve the need and capitalize accordingly.

UNIQUENESS OF ISE

The budding stage of this organization definitely helps the individual to have enough knowledge of any kind of work. And we divulge everyone to a network where they can connect, establish communication and grow.
